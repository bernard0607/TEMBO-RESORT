A Taste of Kenya: Local Dishes to Try

August 15, 2023 By Chef Kamau Culinary, Local Cuisine

Kenyan cuisine is a vibrant fusion of flavors, reflecting the country's diverse cultures and rich agricultural heritage. Here's your guide to must-try local dishes during your stay at Stardom Hotel.

1. Nyama Choma (Grilled Meat)

Kenya's national dish features tender cuts of goat, beef, or chicken marinated in simple spices and slow-grilled to perfection. Best enjoyed with a cold Tusker beer and kachumbari (fresh tomato and onion salad).

2. Ugali & Sukuma Wiki

Ugali, a dense cornmeal porridge, is Kenya's most common staple, served with sukuma wiki (collard greens) in a tomato-based sauce.

Ugali & Sukuma Wiki

Kenya's staple food combination, served in homes and restaurants across the country.

Nyama Choma

Grilled meat, Kenya's favorite dish for celebrations and gatherings.

3. Irio

A traditional Kikuyu dish made from mashed potatoes, peas, corn, and greens, often served with meat dishes or stews.

4. Githeri

This wholesome dish of boiled corn and beans, often cooked with potatoes and carrots, is a staple in central Kenya.

5. Chapati

Introduced by Indian immigrants, these flaky, buttery flatbreads are perfect for scooping up stews and curries.
