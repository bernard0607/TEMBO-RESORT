Welcome to Stardom Hotel!

Stardom Hotel boasts of a unique dining atmosphere away from the hustle and bustle of the city centre. Located along Kasarani Road, Stardom Hotel is just five minutes drive off the prestigious Kasarani Sports Centre, a multi-purpose stadium that was built in 1987 and hosted the All-Africa Games held in Kasarani, Nairobi, Kenya.
