Our Vision

Setting New Standards

Stardom Hotel's successful growth in hospitality has paved the way to host teams from various organizations and missionaries from around the world. Our doors have become the home of many missionaries coming to Kenya to extend a hand to God's people and the needy in the country.

We have also been involved in turning around hospitality ventures, bringing new life to establishments through our managerial expertise and capacity building. We remain a sure source of consultancy services to hotels and hospitality businesses.

Our Mission

Excellence in Hospitality

To provide exceptional hospitality experiences by combining authentic Kenyan warmth with world-class service standards, creating unforgettable stays for our guests while positively impacting our community and environment.
