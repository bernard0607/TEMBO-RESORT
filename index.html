About Us

Tembo Resort & Restaurant
Nairobi's Premier Destination

Tembo Resort & Restaurant is a luxurious 4-star hotel located in the heart of Githurai 45, Nairobi. With our commitment to excellence, we provide world-class accommodation, fine dining, and conference facilities for both business and leisure travelers.

Our prime location near Githurai 45 Sports Centre makes us the perfect choice for sports teams, event attendees, and tourists looking to explore Nairobi's attractions while enjoying first-class hospitality and comfort.
